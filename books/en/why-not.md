**Did I like it?** More or less.

**What it's about:** It's super simple: it tells the story of NotCo from its beginnings up to the book's publication date, in 2023.

**Why I read it:** I saw it mentioned in a reading meetup I didn't attend, but the idea and the book's name stuck with me.

**Favorite part:** Maybe what I enjoyed most was the final section, where you can glimpse all the growth potential the company still has.

**What I didn't like:** In general, the book tries to portray the story of NotCo's founders as an austere path, bordering on the implausible, downplaying their obvious privileges under the guise of "hardships."
