**Did I like it?** Yes.

**What it's about:** Mainly about cash flow management in startups.

**Why I read it:** Someone recommended it to me, although I don't remember who.

**Favorite part:** The technical depth is very good. Business books focused on the financial side often stay on the surface, and this one doesn't.

**What I didn't like:** It's the other side of what I liked most: this is not an introductory book on finance for entrepreneurs. It doesn't even cover traditional companies; it focuses almost exclusively on startups and assumes prior knowledge, so it's not suitable for people who are just getting started.
